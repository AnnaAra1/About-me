REPUBLIC OF ARMENIA

Armenia is a country in the South Caucasus region of Eurasia. It is bordered by Turkey, Georgia, Azerbaijan and Iran. In the 1st century BC Armenia became the first state in the world to adopt Christianity.The official date of state adoption is 301. The modern Republic of Armenia became independent in 1991 during the dissolution of the Soviet Union. On 24 April 1915, Armenian intellectuals were arrested by Ottoman authorities and 1.5mln of Armenians were killed in what has become known as the Armenian Genocide. After which lots of armenians left the county and spread all over the world.
